Delete Your Connect App Account

This page explains how users of Connect App, developed by IWOOD TECHNOLOGIES, can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Connect App on your device.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Once the request is confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted from our active systems.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be removed:

  • Your profile information such as name, email address, and profile details.
  • Authentication information associated with your account.
  • User preferences and application activity linked to your account.

Data Retention

Certain limited information may be retained for legal, financial, or security purposes.

  • Payment transaction records may be retained for up to 1 year for accounting and regulatory compliance.
  • Security logs and system records may be retained for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and security monitoring.

After the applicable retention period, such data will be automatically deleted or anonymized.

Need Help?

If you cannot access the app and wish to request deletion of your account, please contact our support team.
